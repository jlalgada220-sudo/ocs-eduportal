Our Story

Welcome to O.C.S Group of Institute

O.C.S Group of Institute was established with a vision to provide quality education accessible to all. Located in the serene environment of Purbaichapur, our school has been a cornerstone of educational excellence in North 24 Parganas district.

We believe in the holistic development of every child, nurturing not just their academic abilities but also their creative, physical, and emotional well-being. Our experienced faculty, modern infrastructure, and child-centric approach create an ideal environment for learning and growth.

From Kindergarten to Class 6, we provide a strong foundation that prepares students for higher education and life beyond school.
